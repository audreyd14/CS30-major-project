# projext description
I am creating a zombie apocolyse game. The goal of this game will be for the player to escape the zombie-ridden city and survive the journey out. The player will start in the city center, and a map will generate that the player will have to escape. In order to escape the city, the player must collect resources for power ups and health, as well as killing zombies with weapons found around the city. A successful escape will be not turning into a zombie, not running out of health, and finding a way out of the map before the plants overgrow the city. The map will have buildings, plants, and roads to travel on. Over time, the plants will grow onto the roads, which traps the player inside the city
